Who we are

We are Spirit Praise and Worship Centre, a Pentecostal Church. We believe in Jesus Christ as the Lord and Savior and the Holy Bible as the authentic Word of God. We believe and accept the lead of the Holy Spirit and the Demonstration of Power: Speaking in tongues and also being baptized with Holy Spirit and Fire. Our Great Commission is to win Souls for Christ and bring them in the alignment with the Word of God. Changing the World and transforming lives through Moral regeneration through the Holy Spirit and the Word of God.

Our Values

We believe that no matter where you are in your faith, there's always a next you can take to better connect and grow in your relationship with God.
As a church, we are committed to continually growing and encouraging others to spend more time with God, follow Jesus, connect in community, serve others, and live more generously. These are who we want to become.

Our Vision

We are an Antioch type of Church that is led by the Holy Spirit. We believe in Jesus Christ as out Lord and Saviour; and the Holy Bible as the authentic Word of God.
We are commissioned: to win souls for Jesus Christ, disciple the individuals, reach the communities and citied by raising believers into disciples that mature into Ministers and seed churches. We send out ministers to support ministries by touching lost souls with a moral message of the cross. We display the Love of God by assisting the poor, less privileged and the homeless

Pastor Silvester Kalonji

The least of all saints but because of God’s unfailing love and great compassion, found favour in God’s sight and was chosen from his mother’s womb. From a poor and humble family background, Silvester Kalonji was taught all the elementary truths of love from an early age.


By His grace, I am who I am, the least of all His vessels in His Vineyard, yet I am comforted by this truth, God exalts the humble and strengthens the weak. For this I rejoice in my infirmities, knowing that when I am weak then I am strong.”

He chose me and for this I boast in this one thing, I boast in The Lord! I have no life of my own. Christ, my Lord and Saviour is the essence of my life I have done all the wrong things, yet He has forgiven me of all my sins. Unworthy, yet by His unfailing love and great compassion, He has blotted out all my transgressions, washed away all my iniquities and cleansed me from all my sins, He made me acceptable before His sight and now I can boldly approach His throne of grace.
