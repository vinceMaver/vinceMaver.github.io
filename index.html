Who we are

We are Spirit Praise and Worship Centre, a Pentecostal Church. We believe in Jesus Christ as the Lord and Savior and the Holy Bible as the authentic Word of God. We believe and accept the lead of the Holy Spirit and the Demonstration of Power: Speaking in tongues and also being baptized with Holy Spirit and Fire. Our Great Commission is to win Souls for Christ and bring them in the alignment with the Word of God. Changing the World and transforming lives through Moral regeneration through the Holy Spirit and the Word of God.

Our Values

We believe that no matter where you are in your faith, there's always a next you can take to better connect and grow in your relationship with God.
As a church, we are committed to continually growing and encouraging others to spend more time with God, follow Jesus, connect in community, serve others, and live more generously. These are who we want to become.

Giving

Why do we give? Everything we own and everything we have comes from God. So, when we give, we are simply offering Him a small portion of all the abundance He has already given to us. The reason you give is more important than what or how much you give. First, the motivation behind your gift is more important than your gift. This is the point being made in 1 Corinthians 13:3, which says "And though I bestow all my goods to feed the poor, and though I give my body to be burned, and have not charity, it profiteth me nothing. When we give we are not giving to the church but to God through the church.

Tithe and Offerings

"Bring ye all the tithes into the storehouse, that there may be meat in mine house, and prove me now herewith, saith the Lord of hosts, if I will not open you the windows of heaven,and pour you out a blessing, that there shall not be room enough to receive it." Malachi 3:10

The bible teaches that our gifts are first fruits (Prov. 3:9). That means the first thing we do when we receive money is to honor the Lord by giving a portion of it to His work. Anything less is not honoring the Lord.
